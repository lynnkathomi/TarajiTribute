Throughout her career, Taraji P. Henson has received numerous accolades, including an Academy Award nomination, a Golden Globe Award for her role in "Empire," and several Primetime Emmy Award nominations. She has also received recognition for her advocacy work in the mental health field.

Taraji P. Henson is known for her advocacy for mental health awareness, particularly within the African American community. She founded the Boris Lawrence Henson Foundation in honor of her late father, who struggled with mental health issues. The foundation focuses on providing support and resources for those dealing with mental health challenges.
